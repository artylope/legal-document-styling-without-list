Legal Document without using HTML Lists

It is easy to assume that HTML List is the most approriate way to structure a legal document as it has is an ordered list of clauses with nested list and sections. However, after styling several legal documents (Privacy policy, terms of use) for multiple websites. I realise that it is hard to ensure legibility, ordering and structure of a long document with different list-style-types. So I decided to create this, using divs and flexbox.

As you can see, I can use whatever numbering system I want, and it has a consistent indent through out the document. My eyes and heart can now be at peace.
